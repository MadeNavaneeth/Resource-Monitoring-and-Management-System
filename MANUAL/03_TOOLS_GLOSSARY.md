## Question: "I see 'Failed to export' or the dashboard is stuck!"
**Answer:** You might have "Zombie Servers" running in the background.

**The Fix:**
1.  Double-click **`kill_servers.bat`**. (This forces everything to stop).
2.  Then double-click **`run_all.bat`** again.

---

## Question: "The Agent closes immediately on the student laptop!"
**Answer:** It might be crashing.
1.  We fixed this! The new version stays open and shows an error message.
2.  Read the error message. It usually tells you if the IP is wrong.

---

## Question: "I can't see the Dashboard on my phone!"
**Answer:**
1.  Make sure your laptop and phone are on the **Same WiFi**.
2.  Look at the black window on your laptop. It lists "Your Server IP Addresses".
3.  Type that IP (e.g., `http://192.168.1.5:5173`) into your phone's browser.
