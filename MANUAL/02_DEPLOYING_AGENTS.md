# üì° 02. How to Connect Other Laptops (The Students)

**Computer:** On the 20 Lab Computers (or your friend's laptop).

## Step 1: Create the "Agent" File (On YOUR Laptop) before you go to the lab.
Since every WiFi/Lan is different, you need to tell the software "Where are you?".

1.  Connect YOUR laptop to the Network (Same WiFi or LAN as the lab).
2.  Double-click **`build_lab_agent.bat`**.
3.  A black window will ask a question.
    - Type **`y`** and press **Enter**.
4.  Wait 10 seconds.
5.  It will say **"Build Complete!"**.

> **Outcome:** Inside the `dist` folder, there is now a new file called **`agent.exe`**.

---

## Step 2: Copy to Student Laptops (The USB Stick Part)
1.  Take a USB Pen Drive.
2.  Copy the **`agent.exe`** file onto it.
3.  Go to **Student Laptop #1**.
4.  Plug in the USB.
5.  Copy `agent.exe` to their Desktop.

---

## Step 3: Run It! üèÉ
1.  On the Student Laptop, double-click **`agent.exe`**.
2.  A dark window will open.
3.  It will ask for **Device Name** (e.g., "Student-1") and **Server URL**.
    - *The Server URL is usually filled in automatically!*
4.  Click **START**.

**Success!** You will see the charts move, and it will appear on YOUR Dashboard.

*Repeat Step 2 & 3 for all other laptops.*
